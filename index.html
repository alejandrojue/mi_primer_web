EXPERIENCIA

The concept was about big bang theory which shows different products worlds created by a light explotion.

The project “El Limón” consisted of designing a basic scheme made up of five twelve-story towers, on a platform that in turn housed common areas, commercial spaces and parking areas.

The project was modeled in revit. This model worked for blueprints, realistic visualization and quantities. Although the project was modeled before being built, the model was modified during it was building. Because of that, the result was more complete.
